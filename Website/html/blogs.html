Effects of Technology

Technology is a key for development.It's in continuous phase of evolution and is getter better and better which befinits endless number of people all over the world. It has impact in most of the field our live and change life status of people. It has both positive as well as negative impact in our life. Impacts of technology in different sector of our life as follows:

In Business Sector

Technologies has bring a huge change in business standard.Unlike past where people used to travel a long distance for business purpose nowadyas people can simply do it using modern technologies in more significant way.New business models are technology based.Almost all people in business are using required technology for required pupose for implementing business in easy manner.Using proper technology leads to saving of time as well as money so technlogy has bring a great impact for business class.

In Education Sector

We can say effects technology in education is depend upon us that how we are using it.Many researches can be done within very less time using technology whereas students involving in misuse of technology like over gaming, unnecessary net surfing,can leads them them to distract from studies. Along with students teacher are also benifited by technology for instance,they can use modern technology like projector slides instead of traditional black boards and can give large data in just a blink of eyes using pen drives and so on.

In Communication

Use of technology has totally changed the way in communication and at this time has become our necessity and part of our lives. We are using technologies and communicate with each other through emails, faxes, Mobile phones,texting services,video conferences,and social media channels and so on.We can communicate with peoples miles away from us and interact with them visually as well which has turned the whole globe just like a small village.However modern kids are engaged in using technology in improper manner such that they didn't have time to be involved in social activities and time for family which should be controlled by parents.

In Medical Field and Health

Technology in medical fields are very importnat lack of proper technology can lead to death of patients.Many new technologies are developing day by day which can cure diseases that were thaught to be impossible to cure in past.Along with positive impact improper use of technology can have a large impact on users' mental and physical health. Being overly connected can cause psychological issues such as distraction, emotional instability,depression and even memory failure.Beside affecting user's mental health over use of technology can also have negative results on physical health causing vision problems,hearing problems and strain.

TECHNOLOGY has really bring out the huge change.Proper use of it is very necessary for us to be away from negative impact caused by it.Many experts are being involving in developing more and more advanced technology.May be in future such technologies may be developed for which we can only just imagine for now.
